How It Works

How SparkleUp operates — in detail.

From your first signup to your 100th weekly clean — here's exactly how our platform works behind the scenes.

For Clients 🏠

1

You Sign Up & Tell Us Your Needs

Fill in your address, home size, preferred days, and upload your Ghana Card or valid ID for verification.

Your ID is used solely for trust and safety. It's encrypted, stored securely, and never shared with cleaners.
2

We Match You with a Cleaner

Our system matches you with a verified cleaner nearby who is available on your preferred days and has the right experience for your home type.

Matching happens within 24 hours. You'll receive an SMS with your cleaner's name, rating, and first visit date.
3

Your First Clean Happens

Your matched cleaner arrives, follows the SparkleUp checklist, and completes the job. You receive a notification when they arrive and when they finish.

First cleans are treated as a trial. If you're not satisfied, we rematch you within 48 hours at no charge.
4

You Rate the Clean

After every visit you rate your cleaner from 1–5 stars and leave optional notes. This keeps quality high and gives cleaners real feedback.

5

It Repeats — Automatically

Your same cleaner returns every week on your chosen day. Reschedule, pause, or cancel anytime with 24 hours' notice via SMS or WhatsApp.

For Cleaners 🧹

1

You Apply & Submit Your ID

Fill in the cleaner application form with your experience, availability, and area. Upload a valid government ID — Ghana Card, Passport, Voter's ID or Driver's Licence.

Applications are reviewed within 2 business days. Incomplete or unverified applications are not accepted.
2

Background Check & Vetting

Our team runs a background verification using your ID, calls your reference contact, and reviews your experience history.

This typically takes 3–5 business days. We may request a video call or in-person interview for applicants in certain areas.
3

Onboarding & Training

Approved cleaners receive the SparkleUp Standards Guide — our room-by-room cleaning checklist, conduct rules, and safety protocols.

You complete a short online test and a supervised trial clean before going live on the platform.
4

You Receive Job Assignments

Once active, you receive client assignments that match your location and availability. You'll be notified 48 hours before each job.

Cleaners keep a consistent client so relationships and trust can build over time — better for everyone.
5

You Get Paid Weekly

Payment is released every Friday via Mobile Money (MTN MoMo or Vodafone Cash) for all completed cleans that week.

Cleaners with a 4.5+ rating are eligible for SparkleUp Top Rated status and bonus pay on premium jobs.
Trust & Safety

How we keep everyone safe.

Safety is not optional at SparkleUp. Here are the measures we take to protect both clients and cleaners.

🪪

ID Verification for All

Every client and cleaner must submit a valid government-issued ID before using the platform. No exceptions.

🔍

Background Checks

Cleaners undergo a thorough background check before being approved. We verify identity, references, and conduct history.

⭐

Two-Way Rating System

Clients rate cleaners — and cleaners can flag unsafe or disrespectful client situations. Both parties are accountable.

📍

Job Location Logging

Every job is logged with time, location, and cleaner ID. Our team can trace any job within seconds if an issue arises.

💬

24/7 Support Line

Got a concern mid-clean? Our support team is reachable via WhatsApp, SMS or phone — any time of day.

🛡️

Damage Protection Policy

If a cleaner accidentally damages something in your home, SparkleUp's damage policy covers fair resolution and compensation.
