Standards & Duties

What Every SparkleUp Cleaner Does

Our cleaners follow a room-by-room checklist on every single visit. Nothing is left to guesswork — every area has defined tasks and expectations.

🍳 Kitchen

The most-used room — cleaned to food-safe standards.

  • Wipe all countertops and backsplash
  • Clean stovetop burners and hob
  • Wipe outside of oven and microwave
  • Clean sink and taps, remove limescale
  • Wipe cabinet doors and handles
  • Empty and clean bin, replace liner
  • Mop floor thoroughly
  • Wipe fridge exterior and handles

🚿 Bathroom

Sanitised and disinfected on every visit.

  • Scrub toilet bowl, seat, lid and base
  • Clean and disinfect sink and taps
  • Scrub shower and bath, remove soap scum
  • Wipe mirror and glass surfaces
  • Clean tiles and grout lines
  • Mop and disinfect floor
  • Replace toilet paper and hand soap if provided
  • Empty waste bin

🛏 Bedrooms

A clean, calm space for rest and relaxation.

  • Make beds and straighten pillows
  • Dust all surfaces, shelves and headboards
  • Vacuum carpet or mop hard floors
  • Wipe mirrors and picture frames
  • Empty bins
  • Tidy clothes if left out (fold, don't move)
  • Wipe bedside tables and lamps
  • Open windows for ventilation if appropriate

🛋 Living Room

Presentable and dust-free at all times.

  • Vacuum sofas, cushions and under cushions
  • Dust TV, console and electronics
  • Wipe coffee table and side tables
  • Vacuum or mop floors
  • Dust light fittings and ceiling fans
  • Spot-clean walls and skirting boards
  • Arrange cushions and throws neatly
  • Empty bins

🏡 Compound / Outdoor

First impressions start outside.

  • Sweep entrance and walkways
  • Remove cobwebs from exterior walls
  • Wash down veranda or patio
  • Wipe outdoor furniture
  • Clear debris from drains
  • Water plants if instructed

📋 Cleaner Conduct Rules

Professional behaviour is non-negotiable at SparkleUp.

  • Arrive on time, in uniform
  • Never go through personal belongings
  • Report any accidental breakage immediately
  • Respect client's privacy and space
  • No phone use during cleaning hours
  • Lock up properly when leaving
  • Rate every job honestly after completion
Commercial

Office Cleaning Objectives

A clean workspace improves productivity, morale and the impression you make on clients. Our commercial cleaning is built around clear, measurable objectives.

01
Maintain a Hygienic Work Environment
Reduce the spread of bacteria and illness by disinfecting high-touch points daily — keeping staff healthy and productive.
  • Disinfect all door handles and switches
  • Sanitise shared kitchen and break room
  • Clean and disinfect all bathrooms
  • Wipe down shared desks and phones
  • Replenish hand soap and sanitiser stations
02
Sustain a Professional Appearance
First impressions matter. Client-facing areas must always look immaculate — our team ensures reception, meeting rooms and lobbies are pristine.
  • Vacuum and polish reception and lobby areas
  • Clean glass doors and partitions streak-free
  • Arrange furniture neatly after cleaning
  • Ensure meeting rooms are reset and ready
  • Keep plants and décor dusted and tidy
03
Support Staff Productivity
Clutter and dirt are proven to reduce focus. A clean workstation and common area helps your team stay sharp and motivated every day.
  • Empty all desk bins daily
  • Vacuum all workstation areas
  • Clean kitchen appliances (microwave, kettle)
  • Wash dishes left in the sink
  • Remove and dispose of old food from fridge weekly
04
Comply with Health & Safety Standards
Offices have legal cleanliness obligations. SparkleUp's commercial team is trained to meet Ghana Labour Act hygiene standards for workplaces.
  • Document cleaning logs for compliance
  • Use approved, eco-safe cleaning products
  • Ensure wet floor signs are always used
  • Proper disposal of hazardous waste
  • Report maintenance issues (leaks, mould) immediately
